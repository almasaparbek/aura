Favourite books

Favourite books

"Чужак" Стивен Кинг

"The Stranger" is a novel about a man named Terry Maitland, who is accused of a terrible crime — the murder of eleven—year-old Frank Peterson. Irrefutable evidence has been collected against him, but he has a reinforced concrete alibi — on the day of the murder he was in another city in front of hundreds of witnesses.

"Цветы для Элджернона" Дэниел Киз

A mentally retarded person is undergoing an operation to increase intelligence. He becomes a genius, but the effect of the operation is short-lived: the hero loses his mind and ends up in a shelter. The narration is conducted in the first person and is made up of reports written by the main character.

"Зеленая Миля" Стивен Кинг

The book is about a Negro sentenced to death for a murder committed by another, has the gift of healing. He cures the wife of the prison governor from cancer, but this does not save him from execution. The novel is written on behalf of Paul Edgecum, who lives in a nursing home.

"Ұшқан ұя" Бауыран Момышұлы

The novel "Our Family" is a memoir by Bauyrzhan Momyshuly. The work is based on the author's life from childhood, before regaining consciousness, to the threshold of school. Telling that the flying nest contributed to the formation of the writer as a person, he represented family members: grandmother Kyztumas, father Momysh, cousin Momynkul, sisters Ubian, Alimans with their images. The novel "the flying nest" is not only a memoir, but also an ethnographic work reflecting the traditions, customs, customs, and worldview of the Kazakhs.
